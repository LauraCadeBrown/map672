Impending Doom appears

The cartographic gaze is threatened by the deconstruction of the science of cartography. As maps have moved from the spiritual mapping of the human at the center of the earth to this subsequent rise of the human to the ommniscent god-like gaze, a new, third space will arise. The position of god has been deconstructed by the critic and mocked by the programmer. If it is posited that maps are yet another type of text, programmers have taken it one step further. The text dissovles into pixels, into commands, it is all an illusion. There is enlightenment in this meta-understanding, there is power in its manipulation, and there is the matrix, there is code. That it, until it fails...
